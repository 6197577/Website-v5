Why Install a Standby Generator?

Power outages are more than an inconvenience; they can disrupt your life, spoil food, and damage sensitive electronics. A standby generator automatically detects a power outage and restores electricity to your home or business within seconds. It's the ultimate peace of mind.

Our Generator Services Include:

  • Complete Consultation: We help you choose the right size and model for your specific needs.
  • Professional Installation: Our licensed electricians handle the entire process, including the concrete pad, electrical connections, and fuel line hookup.
  • Automatic Transfer Switch: Seamless, automatic switching from utility power to generator power.
  • Maintenance & Repair: We offer service plans to keep your generator in peak condition.
